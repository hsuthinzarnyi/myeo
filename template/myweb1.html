Welcome to Commercial


Early-stage entrepreneurs and start-up business owners can learn what to avoid, as well as what should be included, in their first commercial property lease at a special workshop next week in Tacoma. The City's Community and Economic Development Department is offering a free commercial leasing workshop on Wednesday, May 3, from 6 – 7:15 p.m., at Bates Technical College’s Business Center Auditorium (1101 Yakima Ave. in Tacoma, Room A-130A).

“We’ll talk about what it takes to find the right location, what to ask when considering a long-term commercial leasing contract, and all of the other complexities of commercial leasing,” said Community and Economic Development Director Ricardo Noguera.

"This is a great opportunity for small businesses considering their first brick and mortar location, existing businesses who are thinking about expanding to additional locations, commercial brokers and commercial property owners.”
